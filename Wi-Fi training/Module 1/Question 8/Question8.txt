IEEE:
-> Institute of Electrical and electronics Engeineers.
-> Creates and maintains the 802.11 wireless networking standards
-> Develops technical standards for various technologies. e.g., 802.11 Wi-Fi standards.
-> Covers a wide range of engineering and technology fields.
-> Its members includes Engineers, researchers, and professionals from various industries.

WFA:
-> Wi-Fi Alliance
-> Tests and certifies Wi-Fi devices for compatibility and reliability.
-> Ensures interoperability, security, and certification of Wi-Fi products.
-> Focuses specifically on Wi-Fi technology and branding.
-> Its members includes Wi-Fi equipment manufacturers, service providers, and industry stakeholders.
